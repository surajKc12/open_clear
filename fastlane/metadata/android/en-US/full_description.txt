The OpenClear™ Pass Wallet mobile app provides simple and secure digital credential management, for example for COVID-19 vaccination records and test results. Add a photo of your vaccination card, health test results and other types of data or documents to keep your personal information easily accessible on your mobile phone. Display your records when you’re on the go, traveling through airports or train stations, at retail stores, restaurants, workplaces, schools, businesses, theaters, conferences, sports venues and more.
